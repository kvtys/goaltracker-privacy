Privacy Policy for GoalTracker

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Introduction

GoalTracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

GoalTracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not track your usage, location, or any other data.

Data Storage

All of your goals, activities, and progress data are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services because we do not have servers or use any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

GoalTracker does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric authentication.

Children's Privacy

GoalTracker does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by uninstalling the application or by deleting goals and activities within the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
